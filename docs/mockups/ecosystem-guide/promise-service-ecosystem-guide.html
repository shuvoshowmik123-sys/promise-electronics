Two workspaces, same friendly language

Normal and B2B can share lifecycle wording, but they must never leak into each other.

👤 Normal Customer Workspace

For individual customers. This is where normal Jobs, Pickup/Delivery, Finance verification, and customer portal tracking belong.

1 Service Request or Walk-in record
2 Custody OTP before job creation
3 Normal Job and technician repair feedback
4 Bill, payment verification, delivery OTP

🏢 B2B / Corporate Workspace

For companies, batches, challans, panel-continuous work, and corporate portal work. It stays under B2B.

1 Corporate intake, challan, or batch
2 Corporate jobs and batch tracking
3 Corporate bill, statement, and portal
4 No normal Jobs leakage
!
The simple rule for staff If it is one household customer, use the normal wizard. If it is company, batch, challan, or continuous panel work, use B2B. The system should guide you, not make you guess.

Promise goal: simple to use, hard to misuse.

Each person sees the right work, the right next action, and the right customer status under one roof.
